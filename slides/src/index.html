Introduction to Payment Gateways

In short: A payment gateway is a service to accept credit card payments.

Two types: Modern Gateway and classic Gateway

Payment Integration Workflow

Simple Workflow

1. Shopper places an order and sends credit card information to the payment gateway.

2. Payment gateway will send the encrypted data to the payment processor. The payment processor will reply back to the payment gateway.

3. Order confirmation gets sent out.

Easy, piece of cake, right?

Real Workflow

1. Shopper places an order and sends credit card information to your backend API.

2. Our backend API will send the information to the payment gateway.

3. The Payment gateway will then send the encrypted data to the payment processor.

4. The Payment processor will send the information to the shoppers bank (Issuing Bank).

5. The shoppers bank will check the funds on the card and reply back to the processor.

CVV (Card Verification Value)

A check to validate the 3 digits of the back of the VISA/MC/Discover or 4 digits of the front card of AMEX.
